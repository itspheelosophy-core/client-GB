Cannes Follow-Up Protocol

Gabby Beckford 〰️ Packs Light LLC

Overview

Two paths. Every contact Gabby makes at Cannes flows through one of them.

Select a path to expand
Path A

Landing Page Flow

Contact is directed to the landing page, selects During Cannes or After Cannes, and is routed accordingly

Path B

WhatsApp Capture Flow

Gabby sends name, LinkedIn screenshot, or selfie via WhatsApp. Team preps and executes the next touchpoint.

Contact receives Gabby's landing page link in person, via QR code, or NFC card. They land on the page and self-select their intent.

  • 1 Gabby shares her landing page link in person via QR code, NFC card, or text on the spot
  • 2 Contact lands on the page and sees two clear options: During Cannes and After Cannes
  • 3 If "During Cannes" is selected: Contact is taken down to Gabby's Speaking Engagements section on the page to connect this week
  • 4 If "After Cannes" is selected: Page smooth-scrolls to the contact form further down. Contact fills in their name, email, company, interest area, and how they met Gabby. Auto-confirm sent immediately.
  • 5 All form submissions land in the Cannes 2025 Contact Tracker. Campaign manager reviews within 24 hours, tags by interest and tier, and queues the appropriate follow-up action.

Contact selects "After Cannes" and is scrolled to the contact form. They fill it out and hear from the team within 48 hours.

Gabby sends one of the following via WhatsApp. Team logs the contact immediately and triggers the appropriate next action.

  • Screenshot LinkedIn profile screenshot. Team extracts:
    • Name
    • Title
    • Company
    • LinkedIn URL
  • Name + Info Gabby types "Name, Title, Company, [email or contact]." Team creates the contact record and drafts outreach based on any context Gabby includes.
  • Selfie Photo with the person. Team holds the record until Gabby follows up with context: "we talked about X, follow up about Y." Action is queued from there.
  • Voice Note Gabby records a quick debrief on the person. Team transcribes, extracts the action item, creates the contact record, and confirms back via WhatsApp.

Gabby or the team selects one or more actions based on contact type and on-ground discussions.

Gabby selects and initiates the action directly

☕ Lunch or Coffee Invite

Warm, personal email to meet IRL or virtually. Casual tone, specific hook from the conversation.

🔗 LinkedIn Connect

Connection request with a short personalized note referencing Cannes or the shared context.

📹 Virtual Coffee

Calendar link with a short personal hook. 20-minute format, no hard agenda, just continuing the conversation.

📨 Topic Follow-Up

Reference the specific thing discussed. Share a relevant resource, insight, or direct ask tied to that conversation.

🔔 Set Reminder

Tag a follow-up date in the tracker. No immediate outreach needed. Revisit when timing or opportunity aligns.

📄 Send Collab Materials

Share media kit, speaker one-sheet, or capabilities deck relevant to the contact's brand or role.

Every contact captured via either path gets a record in the Cannes 2025 Contact Tracker. Fields below are required at minimum.

Full Name First + last. Note preferred name if different.
Title / Company Job title and brand or organization.
Contact Info Email, LinkedIn URL, or both.
Source Landing page form · WhatsApp screenshot · Name drop · Selfie · Voice note
Interest Tag Partnership · Speaking · Press · Community · General
Tier A — follow immediately · B — nurture · C — passive watch
Action Taken LinkedIn sent · Email drafted · Email sent · Call booked · Materials sent
Next Step + Date Next follow-up action and target send date.
Notes Context from the conversation, topics discussed, mutual connections, anything Gabby flagged via WhatsApp.
